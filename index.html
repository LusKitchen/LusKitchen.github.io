Accounting for Altitude

The higher you are, the lower the air pressure. Affecting interactions of ingredients like flour, fats, liquid, and more. Different types of baked goods need different adjustments to high-altitude baking. To find out if you need to adjust your recipe, look up ingredients and how they are affected. You might also need to do some trial and error to prefect high-altitude baking.
