Ingredients

  • 4 pounds yellow potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup melted salted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Peel and quarter potatoes. Put in a pot of cold heavily salted water.
  2. Add garlic and bring to a boil. Cook uncovered for 15 minutes or until fork-tender. Drain and let sit in the pot until most of the water has evaporated.
  3. Heat milk in microwave until warm. Add butter and milk to potatoes then mash. Add salt and pepper.
