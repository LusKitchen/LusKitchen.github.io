Ingredients

  • 4 overly ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a bread loaf pan with butter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Mash bananas and add to batter. Add eggs and mix.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix, then add vanilla and chocolate chips.
  5. Pour batter into pan and bake 50â€“60 minutes.
  6. Cool for 15 minutes before removing.
