Ingredients

  • 2 pounds yellow or red potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 424 degrees and line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Clean potatoes and cut into 1-inch cubes. Soak in cold water for 20 minutes to an hour to remove starch. Pat dry with paper towel.
  3. Toss potatoes with olive oil, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread on baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes until tender.
