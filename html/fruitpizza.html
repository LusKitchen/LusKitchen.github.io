Fruit Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup crisco
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 egg
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • fresh and canned fruit
  • 1/2 cup fruit juice
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a circular pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together butter, crisco, and sugar. Add flour, salt, cream of tartar, and egg. Mix until combined into a dough.
  3. Pat dough evenly on the bottom of the pan. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Take out and cool completely.
  4. Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar in a small bowl. When crust is cooled, spread over the crust evenly.
  5. Arrange fruit on the prepared crust to your preference. Save fruit juice from the canned fruit. Use a small pot on medium-low heat, pour in fruit juice and add cornstarch. Mix constantly until thick glaze like consistency.
  6. Drizzle glaze over the prepared pizza and serve.
