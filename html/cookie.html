Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and grease baking sheets.
  2. Cream butter and sugars together until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla. In a small bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix in dry ingredients until combined.
  3. Mix in chocolate chips.
  4. Roll 2 tablespoons of dough onto the baking sheet.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes. Take out when sides just start turning brown.
  6. Let sit on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing.
