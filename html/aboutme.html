Bio

I am a sophomore at Western Colorado University. I am currently enrolled in a Computer Science class and my final project is to make a website. I decided to make a recipe website because I bake and cook a lot. Something that always annoys me while on a recipe website is how crowded they are with ads, pop-ups, and unnecessary information. I wanted to make a simple website that will get you to the recipe you want in seconds.
