Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 overly ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a bread loaf pan with butter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Mash bananas with a fork until desired consistency and add to the batter. Add in eggs and lightly whisk. Mix until blended.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until blended. Add in vanilla extract and chocolate chips, mix until combined.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let rest for 15 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack or eating.
