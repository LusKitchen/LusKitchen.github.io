Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Crumb Topping

  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

    • Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line pan with paper liners.
    2. In a mixing bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    3. Add butter and egg, stir until combined. If batter is thick, add 1 tbsp of milk until you have a smooth consistency.
    4. Fold in blueberries and pour into liners, filling them to the top.
    5. For crumb topping, combine flour, sugar, butter, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Mix with hands or fork until crumbly. Sprinkle batter with crumb topping
    6. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
